Dormer Conversions

This is one of the most popular loft conversions. These kind of house modernizations are performed in the place of the slope of the existing roof. We can install normal or conventional windows. This house extension provides more living space for us. Dormer conversion is very popular as it enhances the house magnificently. Mansard loft conversions

This type of house modernization is dedicated mostly to the terraced houses and involves raising walls (walls between neighbours). One external wall slopes gently inwards but the roof stays flat. This is the most demanding and expensive loft conversion. Lots of construction work must be done. Mansard conversion significantly increases available living space and adds value to the house. Mansards conversions always require planning permissions. Fits best with older properties.

Hip to gable loft conversions

This type of loft conversion is dedicated to detached houses. It is also perfect for the end of a terrace. A hip to gable loft conversion straightens an inwardly slanted ended roof to create a vertical wall. If You have a hip roof, hip to gable loft conversion is for You. This loft conversion always requires a planning permission. Ideal for summer house and bungalows.

Roofing light Loft conversions
