Discover our long history....

From an early age, the owner of the FIX Sco LTD helped run a family construction company, founded by his grandfather and father. He inherited the technical skills and talent, which had been passed down from generation to generation, using them to fuel his passion for continuing the family legacy.
Naturally, his career in the construction industry took off extraordinarily quickly. He started as a site assistant and ended as a supervisor, managing a large group of construction workers for one of the major players in the building and renovation industry within Scotland.

Company "FIX Sco LTD" started in 2006. In the beginning, we were a small, two-person business, specializing in basic renovation and construction services such as plumbing, painting, carpentry and small home repairs in Glasgow, Edinburgh and the surrounding areas. Over the years, we gained the experience and necessary competencies required to expand. Thanks to that, we have developed the scope of our services, with countless satisfied customers who can vouch for us. With time, we have completed hundreds of both small and large commercial renovation and building developments. From year to year, we significantly increased our revenues and the volume of employment. A turning point for our renovation company was the change of its capability. From a small family business, we became a LTD company.

We provide a whole range of home renovation services.
